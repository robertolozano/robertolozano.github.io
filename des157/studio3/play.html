Game Rules:


Each player will play as a pokemon. At each round you will have 2 options:

1. Use Turn
-If you hit, then the opponent will lose health.
-If you miss, then it becomes the opponent’s turn
-If your Pokemon becomes distracted, the opponent has time to heal, and it becomes their turn.

2. Quit Game
-You will go back to menu screen and game will end.

First to deplete opponent’s health wins!
